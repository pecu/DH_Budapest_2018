FOMC Delivers "Dovish Hike", Lays Out Plans For Balance-Sheet Unwind
2017-06-14 20:01:00+00:00
in the most well-telegraphed, 'never in doubt, no matter how bad the economic data is' fomc statement ever, the fed hiked rates by 25bps and maintained its rate-hike trajectory forecast, shrugging off the collapse in economic data (including weak inflation). the market was anticipating a so-called 'dovish hike' and the fed delivered by saying it is "monitoring inflation developments closely" and also offered more detailed plans of the balance sheet unwind (beginning this year). interesting hedgeing against the chance of a "no rate hike" was "aggressive" today in fed funds futures. here are
